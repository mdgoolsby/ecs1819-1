Influencers

Parents

When Grace Hopper was asked who influenced her, she said "My mother's very great interest in mathematics and my father's, a house full of books, a constant interest in reading, and insatiable curiosity... these were a primary influence all the way along."I was unable to find photos of her parents so I have left a question mark instead.

What she is famous for

Grace Hopper

Grace Hopper was part of the team that helped program the Mark 1. The Mark 1 was one of the first computors and was created at Harvard. She was the only female who worked on the computer. She is considered "a pioneer of computer programming who invented one of the first compiler related tools."
